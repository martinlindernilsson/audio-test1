Listening test

You are about participate in a listening test in which you will be able to alter the characteristics of sound clips in various ways with a slider. You will be asked to find a position on the slider where you can follow the speech as clearly as possible while still maintaining a satisfactory overall listening experience.

Each sound clip is an approximately 20 second long outtake from Swedish Radio, SR, and will be played in a loop when started. When deciding on your preferred position on the slider, please take the entirety of the sound clip into consideration.

For each sound clip, note your resulting number in the Google form. You might want to keep both windows open side by side to make this process easier.

The test consists of __ sound examples and will take approximately 10 minutes.
